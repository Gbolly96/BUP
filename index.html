Why Choose Us

The BIUP
Standard

We don't just sell tyres. We run an end-to-end supply chain — from import and warehousing to serial-tracked delivery and post-sale service.

01
Real-Time Stock Visibility
Our unified inventory system tracks every serial number across our Main Yard and all warehouses. You always know exactly what's available before you order.
02
Electronic Proof of Delivery
Three-party sign-off on every dispatch — inventory officer, rider, and customer. Digital confirmation the moment your tyres are received.

Credit Facilities

Official distributor for Bridgestone, Michelin, Goodyear, Hankook, Continental, Dunlop, Pirelli, and more. Every tyre authentic and certified.
